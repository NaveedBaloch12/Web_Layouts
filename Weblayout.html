Multan, city, south-central Punjab province, east-central Pakistan. It is built on a mound just east of the Chenab River. The chief seat of the Malavas, an ancient people who ruled the region in the 4th century bce, Multan was subdued by Alexander the Great in 326 bce and was brought under Umayyad rule about 712 ce.
